# Trip Plan: Rome, Italy

## Travelers
- Group: 2 adults
- Dates: October 24-31, 2026 (7 nights)

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Saturday, October 24 - Arrival
- Morning: Arrive at FCO (~8:00 AM)
- Morning: Transfer to Hotel Artemide
- Late Morning: Explore Via Nazionale
- Afternoon: Lunch near hotel

### Day 2: Sunday, October 25 - Colosseum & Roman Forum
- Morning: Colosseum Tour ($100 for 2)
- Afternoon: Roman Forum & Palatine Hill
- Evening: Dinner near Piazza Venezia, Trevi Fountain

### Day 3: Monday, October 26 - Vatican Museums
- Morning: Vatican Museums & Sistine Chapel ($80 for 2)
- Afternoon: St. Peter's Basilica
- Evening: Dinner in Prati neighborhood

### Day 4: Tuesday, October 27 - Trastevere Food Tour
- Morning: Free morning
- Afternoon: Trastevere Food Tour ($170 for 2)
- Evening: Explore Trastevere, dinner at trattoria

### Day 5: Wednesday, October 28 - Pantheon & Shopping
- Morning: Pantheon, Piazza Navona, Campo de' Fiori
- Afternoon: Shopping (Via del Corso)
- Evening: Rooftop Aperitivo, dinner near Spanish Steps

### Day 6: Thursday, October 29 - Appian Way & Cooking Class
- Morning: Explore Appian Way
- Afternoon: Pasta & Tiramisu Cooking Class ($190 for 2)
- Evening: Final dinner

### Day 7: Friday, October 30 - Departure
- Morning: Check-out, final shopping
- Afternoon: Transfer to FCO
- Morning: Depart DL247 at 9:00 AM
