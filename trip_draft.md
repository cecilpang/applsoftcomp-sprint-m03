## Travel Tips

### Transportation
- **MTR (Metro):** Fastest way around; use Octopus card (reloadable) for convenience
- **Buses:** Extensive network; slower but reaches more areas
- **Star Ferry:** Iconic ferry between Kowloon and Central; cheap and scenic ($2.50 HKD)
- **Taxis:** Readily available; red (urban) and green (New Territories)
- **Ferries:** To outlying islands (Lantau, Lamma)

### Currency
- **Currency:** Hong Kong Dollar (HKD)
- **Exchange Rate:** ~7.80 HKD per 1 USD
- **Exchange:** Exchange at airports or ATM; most places accept credit cards
- **Tipping:** Not expected but appreciated (10% at restaurants)

### Customs
- No visa required for US citizens (90-day limit)
- No currency restrictions
- Declare: liquor, tobacco, merchandise for commercial purposes
- Prohibited: narcotics, weapons, CBD products
- Duty-free: 1L alcohol, 60 cigarettes

### General Tips
- **Language:** Cantonese main; English widely spoken in tourist areas
- **Weather:** Hot/humid June (28-32°C); bring light clothing, sunscreen
- **Electricity:** 220V/50Hz; bring adapter (US plug won't fit)
- **Shopping:** Tsim Sha Tsui, Central, Causeway Bay
- **Peak Tram:** Book online to avoid queues

## Travel Warnings/Restrictions

### US Department of State Advisory (Level 2 - Exercise Increased Caution)
- **Issued:** November 27, 2024
- **Reason:** Arbitrary enforcement of local laws

### Key Warnings:
1. **National Security Law:** Broad vaguely-defined offenses (secession, subversion, terrorism, collusion)
2. **Political Activity:** Avoid demonstrations, political statements, social media criticism
3. **Surveillance:** Visitors may be monitored; hotel rooms may be searched
4. **Drug Laws:** Zero tolerance; severe penalties including life imprisonment or death
5. **Dual Nationals:** May face additional scrutiny; limited consular access

### Recommendations:
- Enter Hong Kong on US passport
- Keep passport with you at all times
- Avoid demonstrations and political gatherings
- Do not photograph protesters or police
- Monitor local media for updates
- Enroll in STEP (Smart Traveler Enrollment Program)

### Health Advisory:
- June: Poor air quality typical
- No vaccinations required
- Medical facilities good; insurance recommended
